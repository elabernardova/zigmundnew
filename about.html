Lubos Zigmund is a fashion designer based in Prague. He has started his studies in 2016 at College of Fashion Design in Prague and currently he is launching his own brand ZIGMUND focused on menswear and accessories. In 2019 he took part in fashion contest #jetovtobe organised by Vodafone in collaboration with Mercedes-Benz Prague Fashion Week. In the same year, he is going to finish his studies with the final fashion show, where he is going to present his latest collection inspired by working class.
